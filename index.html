Our Heritage Chicken Breeds

We raise a variety of heritage chicken breeds chosen for their hardiness, temperament, and exceptional meat and egg quality.

Rhode Island Red

A hardy American breed known for excellent egg production and good meat quality. Our Rhode Island Reds are dual-purpose birds with rich, flavorful meat.

Dual Purpose Excellent Layers Cold Hardy

Barred Plymouth Rock

An American heritage breed with distinctive black and white barred feathers. Known for their docile temperament and excellent meat quality.

Dual Purpose Docile Meat Quality

Buff Orpington

A British breed with beautiful golden feathers. These gentle giants are known for their friendly disposition and rich, tender meat.

Golden Feathers Very Docile Premium Meat

Black Australorp

An Australian breed with glossy black feathers. These birds are excellent egg layers that also produce delicious, tender meat.

Glossy Black Prolific Layers Dual Purpose

Silver Laced Wyandotte

An American breed with stunning silver-laced plumage. These birds are excellent foragers that produce flavorful meat with a distinctive taste.

Silver Laced Good Foragers Winter Hardy

Our Farm Story

Established in 1978, Aglow Farms began with a simple mission: to raise chickens the way nature intended. What started as a small backyard flock has grown into a thriving family operation spanning three generations.

Our commitment to sustainable farming practices has never wavered. We believe that happy, healthy chickens raised on pasture produce the most nutritious and delicious meat and eggs.

Today, our farm spans 75 acres of rolling pastures where our heritage breed chickens roam freely, foraging for insects and plants that contribute to their exceptional flavor. We supplement their natural diet with locally-sourced organic grains free from GMOs, antibiotics, and hormones.
